Brief History of Rock Music

Rock music originated in the 1950s and evolved through many styles and generations. From classic rock bands like Led Zeppelin to modern indie rock groups like The Killers, the genre continues to influence global music culture.

In the 1990s, grunge bands like Nirvana changed the sound of rock, while the 2000s brought indie and alternative rock into the mainstream.
